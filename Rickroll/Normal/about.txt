Normal (Just runs once), and keeps the volume at 100%.
Can be stopped with (in cmd or powershell by running these commands)
_taskkill /f /im python.exe
taskkill /f /im powershell.exe_
or task manager, though it will still start after a reboot of the computer.

The "Fast" version of the two files just does all the things the normal one does, but in a one line command (faster).
